Dream Destinations

It is quite nice to have a place of peace and excitement to want to visit or explore.
  • placeVenice (Italy)

    Venice has only canals where people take a vaporetta(a waterbus) along the city. This is an interesting place to visit, moving around the city only along water canals, which is not my regular way of life. They have beautiful attractions too and you can explore alongside the vaporetta rides. The Grand Canal is the largest canal and Venice is a historic place with beautiful architecture. Visiting the place would take you to the colonial masters period and experience enormous amount of history and culture.
  • placeBali (Indonesia)

    Bali is one of my favorite relaxation destinations. To experience the serenity that comes with the land, take in the beauty of nature and to experience a different culture is enough for me. They have exotic hotels and numerous activities to keep you eneterained. Bali is a bucketlist destination for me and we will be going soon.
  • placeBurj Khalifa (Dubai)

    The Burj Khalifa is the highest building in the world so imagine looking from the height! It also houses numerous shopping centers which is quite an attraction. Generally, Dubai is a facinating place, in terms of how they have turned a once desert filled country into an industrial and commercial hub. A visit to such an advance place would be quite intriguing.
  • placeThe Giraffe Manor (Kenya)

    The Giraffe Manor is a hotel that guests get to have breakfast with giraffes. They have made the breakfast room windows so large and open that the giraffes get to peep through to be served breakfast by the guests. It will be a nice experience to serve food to these fascinating creatures with such long necks. These Rothschild's giraffes are not domestic, they go back into the wild after the visit.
  • placeBora Bora and The Maldives

    Bora Bora and the Maldives are both relaxation destinations for me. The serene atmosphere and the unique natural features of the land are just so beautiful. Both of these places have.
  • placeSerengeti National Park (Tanzania)

    Generally, Safari rides are organised through this park to witness these animals in their natural settings. The Serengeti park is a habitat to a number of wild animals like Zebra, Lion, Tigers, Rhinoceros, Leopards, Hyenas and various mammals. It is an area for the largest migration of different mammals in between Tanzania and Kenya. It is quite a bucketlist experience to see these wild animals.
  • placeThe Northern Lights (Norway)

    I have always been fascinated about the sky and its beautiful formations day in and day out. On discovering that, there is a time and place in the world where the sky sometimes goes green, red, blue, pink, yellow and acts as lights too, I was amused. The Northen Lights are so beautiful. There are hotels with transparent room ceilings so you can see these lights from the comfort of your room. It occurs in places like New Zealand, Sweden, Norway and Antarctica.
  • placeHigher Ground Restaurant (South Africa)

    I first saw the restaurant on a TV show where they allowed the contestants to experience this breathtaking experience. I immediately added it to my bucketlist. Who decides to put a restaurant with waiters too in the skies just for the fun of it? Well, I am definitely booking me a seat sometime for the perks that come with staying in the skies for that long and eat too as you there.
  • placeWhere the Desert meets the Ocean (Namibia)

    The Skeleton Coast as they call it, is where the desert meets the ocean. This is quite amazing to have two distinct landforms just next to each other. It would be nicer to make an aerial trip over the distance to take in the complexity of this natural occurrence. Nature is just beautiful, so anything out of the norm is a plus and an attraction for me.

There is adventure in the unknown.
