Meet Barbara

In my childhood, travelling and exploring attraction sites has always been something that my parents did, which grew with me as well. Ever since I was a kid, my dad travelled with us, to a lot of intersting places and encouraged us to be comfortable in different environments and learn about these places as much as we can. Travelling makes you more open-minded, understand different cultures and experience a lot of exciting life changing perspectives..At age 27, I still get the excitement of packing and looking forward to the adventure that awaits as I was since age 12. The journeys I have been on so far has made me always look outside the box, look beyond the situation when it presents itself. You realise, there is more to life than what we experience in our daily schedules. Travelling is quite insightful and more knowledge-based in an indirect form. There are more things to be learnt when we experience them.

Curiosity

Ever wondered why people do what they do? The interest they have towards their desires are very strong that they keep doing it over and over again. It is the same with travel enthusiasts like me. There are countless experiences and lessons only traveling can teach us. A way to escape from the daily life stress is always also a priority for me. The feeling in breathing a new air, eating different dishes, dressing up like the natives and participating in daring activities are some of the things that being at home will not give you. I would encourage everyone to travel because more life awaits in places unknown.

There is adventure in the unknown.
