Aloha

Welcome to my favorite thing to do aside being at home and consuming a lot of documentaries, travelling. There is nothing more exciting than discovering new places and taking part in the adventures that come with it.

Why Travel?

  • Making Memories

    Experiencing places and being in the moments creates a wide variety of memories. Life is more of memories than acquiring stuff. Travels gives us more memories to live with and share with our friends. These memories stay with us for the rest of our lives and makes us enjoy life better.

  • Opening Minds

    Experiencing different cultures, people, places and food as we travel enlightens our minds and changes our preconceived ideas. It creates opportunites for us to learn from these experiences. Other times, it changes the way we live our lives indirectly as we get to understand the way others live and why they believe what they do. Travel simply grows in knowledge and intellect.

  • Sense of Appreciation

    Many places are distinct in culture based on their rights, resources and practices that helps us appreciate our own lives. Many timeS, we take things for granted since we have not experienced any other situation aside our own. When we experience the less privileged who are still able to make life happen regardless, we learn to be grateful in our abundance.

  • New Experiences

    There is nothing more eye-opening than surrounding yourself with another culture that chooses to live their life completely different from yours. Especially in their way of dressing, language, food, architecture among others. They say, routine is the killer of the soul. Experience something new is better and more refreshing.

  • Why Not

    There is more to this world than the small corner God put us. So why not take the advantage to explore the other side? Why be stuck in one town or city for the rest of your life? There is more to this than our immediate surroundings, so i would say, go for it. So, why not?
